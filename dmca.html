DMCA Policy | Murder-unblocked.github.io

Last Updated: May 27, 2025

This DMCA Policy explains how Murder-unblocked.github.io (https://murder-unblocked.github.io) handles notifications of alleged copyright infringement in accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act ("DMCA").

1. Copyright Respect

We respect the intellectual property rights of content creators and respond to valid DMCA takedown notices that comply with applicable U.S. copyright law.

2. Filing a Takedown Notice

If you believe that your copyrighted material is being used on our site without permission, please send a DMCA notice including the following details:

  • Your full name and contact information (email, address, phone)
  • A description of the copyrighted material being infringed
  • The exact URL where the infringing material appears
  • A statement of good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the information is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Submit this information through the contact method listed in our footer. Incomplete or non-compliant requests may be ignored.

3. Counter Notice Procedure

If you believe a takedown was made in error, you may submit a counter notification including:

  • Your name and contact details
  • Identification of the content removed and its original location
  • A sworn statement of good faith belief that the content was removed in error
  • Your consent to jurisdiction of a U.S. Federal Court in California
  • Your signature

4. Repeat Infringer Policy

Users found to repeatedly infringe on copyright may have their access restricted or permanently blocked at our discretion.
